1. What is the target audience?

	medical patients who want their meds delivered for various reasons 
		(ex transportation, psychological, immobility issues)

2. What problem that is solved?

	medical prescription delivery system for those who cannot get their medication themselves

3. What is the smallest version:

	Gen 1:
	user input fields = name, prescription number, pharmacy address, delivery address.
	driver input = name, pickup status.
	site functions = 
		takes in user input;
		takes in driver input;
		calculates delivery time;
		updates when item is received;
